Required Params :

1) heading="" (Title of Notification).
2) messege="" (Notification Messege).
3) flag ="" one of any apps

  • IUEC
  • FOP
  • NFOP
EX = /notification?messege=hello&heading=h1&flag=IUEC


Additional Params :

1) . data=""  to pass Additonal Data to Show in App Popup

Ex :http://localhost:5000/notification?messege=hello&heading=h1&flag=IUEC&data=1121212


1) . date =""  to send on specific date
2) . time=""   to Send at Specific Time
(IF Time is Not MEntioned It Will send At 9:00 am UTC)
3) . tz ="" Time zone In which To mentioned
if Not mentioned Will Set +00 , Follow Below Tabel For UTC

Ex :http://localhost:5000/notification?messege=hello&heading=h1&flag=IUEC&data=1121212&date=April 22 2018&time=9:00:00 pm&tz=IST1*ngFor="let item of stream | async as list"
